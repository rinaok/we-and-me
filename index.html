We & Me is a global real-estate company that promotes rental accommodation to member tenants through co-living to combine both a luxurious life style with community-based living, adding value (and income) through its unique concept of social networking.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to combine professional and social living in one place, so that our member tenants will get to enjoy globally facilities, such as access to an open workspace, a cinema room and to gyms.
